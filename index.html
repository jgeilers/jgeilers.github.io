About

I care about the future of digital safety, technology governance, and how emerging systems affect society. My work spans cybersecurity, trust & safety, geopolitics, AI, and open-source intelligence. I get excited about translating complex systems into practical tools for people who need them most.

Previously I helped publish The Long Fuse, a comprehensive report on online misinformation during the 2020 U.S. election, partnered with Smart Parks to build technology that supports wildlife conservation, and co-founded a civic tech startup to help communities engage with their elected officials.

In between projects you can find me outside—often in Minnesota's boundary waters—rock climbing, running, or trading book recommendations. I am always searching for stories that help us imagine better futures.
